About Metro

Metro Property Care is the dedicated e‑commerce and national distribution extension for Icon Iron Rail—bringing proven fabrication quality from Detroit to contractors, builders and property professionals across the United States.

We operate as a third‑party subcontracted partner so that Icon Iron Rail can focus on fabrication and local storefront transactions. Metro extends access to their product line beyond the local market. We sell iron products and building components by part through a streamlined online store, with reliable packaging, shipping coordination, and contractor‑friendly ordering—so projects stay on schedule.

Product Catalog

  • Wrought Iron Railings — standard sizes and custom configurations for homes and commercial properties.
  • Step Rails — standard‑size step rails designed for common stair and porch applications; contractor‑ready.
  • Custom Gates — fabricated gates for security and curb appeal (residential and commercial).
  • Iron Fencing & Sections — fence panels/sections and compatible components.
  • Custom Fabricated Parts — CNC plasma cutting and custom‑cut components for builders, integrators and metalwork projects.
  • Accessories & Hardware — mounting kits, fasteners, post shoes, decorative caps and project add‑ons.
